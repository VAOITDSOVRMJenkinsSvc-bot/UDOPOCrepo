First Notice of Death

Death of a Beneficiary Receiving Benefits in His/Her Own Right (i.e., widow, helpless child, dependent parent, Chapter 35 child) reported

    I'm sorry for your loss. I know this is a difficult time and we will do everything we can to help you today.
    PCR must complete FNOD 0820a and attach to an encrypted email and route to ROJ via PCT mailbox for award action. In the subject line, enter "Immediate Award Action Required."
    PCR must discuss the status of the payment and advise that the benefit will be terminated the first day of the month in which the individual died. Also, if applicable, PCR must discuss the potential for burial in a National Cemetery as shown on Death Related Checklist. Suggested scripting is provided below:
  • The benefit payment for the month in which the individual passed away, in addition to any benefit payments that are received after his/her passing, will need to be returned.
  • We'd like to make you aware that (insert name of deceased) may be eligible for burial in a National Cemetery. Would you like information on this benefit?
  • If contact information is requested, please give telephone number for the appropriate National Cemetery and advise that he/she may contact the funeral director for information. After providing the contact information, PCR must offer to transfer the call.

Death of a Dependent of a Live Veteran with an Award reported

    I'm sorry for your loss. I know this is a difficult time and we will do everything we can to help you today.
    If the account is eligible to be transferred to a Dependency Pilot Agent for Processing:

  • I would like to transfer you to a specialist who can assist with updating our records. May I place you on a short hold to transfer you to a specialist?
    Dependency Pilot Agent:
    After taking the necessary action to process the removal of the deceased dependent, provide the caller with information on the potential eligibility for burial in a National Cemetery for the deceased dependent. Remember to offer condolences during the interaction.
  • We'd like to make you aware that your spouse may be eligible for burial in a National Cemetery. Would you like information on this benefit?
  • If contact information is requested, please give telephone number for the appropriate National Cemetery and advise that he/she may contact the funeral director for information. After providing the contact information, PCR must offer to transfer the call.
    If the account is not eligible to be transferred to a Dependency Pilot Agent for Processing and all necessary actions have been taken to report the death of the dependent:
  • We'd like to make you aware that your (spouse, child, parent) may be eligible for burial in a National Cemetery. Would you like information on this benefit?
  • (If contact information is requested, please give telephone number for the appropriate National Cemetery and advise that he/she may contact the funeral director for information). After providing the contact information, PCR must offer to transfer the call.

Death of Veteran (recent) reported

    I'm sorry for your loss. I know this is a difficult time and we will do everything we can to help you today.
    PCR must:
  • Take the necessary actions to process the FNOD in CRM/UD, to include the MOD and PMC if appropriate
  • Complete FNOD 0820a and send to appropriate ROJ via encrypted email
  • Address any directly asked benefits questions
  • Address burial benefits if the date of death is within 3 days of date of contact
  • Offer to provide the caller with a package of information to address available death benefits
  • Provide information on the status of the Veteran's payment/award
