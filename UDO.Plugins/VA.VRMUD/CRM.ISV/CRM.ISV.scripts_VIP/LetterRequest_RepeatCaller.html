Qualifications for Using Letter Request:  Repeat Caller Script:

  • System of records clearly indicates that caller is a repeat caller,
  • System of records confirms that previous PCR prepared and sent the requested letter, and
  • Caller is inquiring about status of previously requested letter and not requesting a new letter or new delivery method.
    • For example, caller originally requested an amounts letter by mail.  The repeat caller script can be used if the caller is requesting an update on the status of the amounts letter that was sent by mail.  However, the repeat caller script can not be used if the caller is requesting a different type of letter or requesting that the letter be resent by another means, i.e., by fax.

Script

In reviewing your record, I see that you contacted us on (insert date of last contact) to request a (insert type of letter).  The requested letter was prepared and sent on (insert date letter was mailed).  Please allow 7 to 10 days for the letter to be received. May I please confirm your address to ensure our records are up to date? 

  • PCR should confirm mailing address in system of records and take appropriate action to update as needed.

 

Components

  • Date of previous contact
  • Confirm type of letter originally requested
  • Date requested letter was sent
  • Advise to allow 7 to 10 days from the date letter was sent for the letter to be received
  • Confirm address and take appropriate action to update

 

Script Notes:

  • For the purposes of script qualifications, a “repeat caller” is considered to be a caller who has contacted the VA within the last 7 days requesting information on the same issue.
  • When handling a letter request call, PCRs should review the system of records to determine if letter has been previously requested prior to taking action to initiate the requested letter.  If the caller meets the “repeat caller” requirement, PCR can utilize the above script and advise appropriately.
  • If caller is adamant that they want or need another letter, PCR is expected to prepare and send the letter as requested.
