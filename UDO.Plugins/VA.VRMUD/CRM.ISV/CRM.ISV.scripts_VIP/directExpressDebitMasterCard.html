Direct Express® Debit MasterCard® Card Call Center Script

    The U.S. Department of the Treasury will send a direct mail letter in May and June to VA Compensation or Pension check recipients in seven states to encourage them to switch from a paper check to the Direct Express® Debit MasterCard® card for their payments. The Direct Express® card is endorsed by VA and is safer, easier, and more convenient than receiving a paper check.

Q. I received a letter in the mail about the Direct Express card. Is it legitimate?

    A. Yes. A letter was sent to VA Compensation or Pension check recipients in these seven states - Texas, New York, California, Mississippi, Kentucky, Georgia and West Virginia - to encourage you to switch from a paper check to the Direct Express® card. The Direct Express® card is endorsed by VA and is safer, easier, and more convenient than receiving a paper check.

Q. Can I sign up over the phone right now?

    A. I'm sorry, I can't sign you up for the card here, but you can sign up by calling a US Treasury phone line dedicated specifically for your enrollment at 1-(888) 544-6347. You can also complete and return the application that was included with your letter.

Q. Can I sign up online?

    A. No, but you can sign up by calling 1-(888) 544-6347. You can also complete and return the application that was included with your letter.

Q. I am a fiduciary. Can I sign up to get the Direct Express card?

    A. The card is not yet available for fiduciaries receiving VA Compensation or Pension benefit payments.

Q. Where can I get more information about the Direct Express card?

    A. Please visit www.USDirectExpress.com. for more information about card fees and features, Please note that online sign-up is not yet available for VA Compensation or Pension beneficiaries. The best way to get information about signing up is to call 1-(888) 544-6347.
