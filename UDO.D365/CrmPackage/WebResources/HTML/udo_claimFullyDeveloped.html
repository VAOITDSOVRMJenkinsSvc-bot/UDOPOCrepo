Fully Developed Claim (FDC) script

Issue:

In the event that a caller requests adding a new disability to their pending Fully Developed claim, please provide the following information.

Script:

Any disability can be claimed at any time. However, adding a new disability or requesting private evidence to be obtained in support of your claim, will remove your claim from the Fully Developed Claim program. Claims in this program are generally expedited and reach completion in a shorter timeframe than through the traditional claims process. I am happy to add {new disability} to your pending claim should you choose to have it processed traditionally and not in the expedited Fully Developed Claim program.

 

Script Issued April 16, 2013
