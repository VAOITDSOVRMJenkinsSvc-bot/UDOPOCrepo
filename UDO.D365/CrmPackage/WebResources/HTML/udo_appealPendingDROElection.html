Appeal Status = Appeal Pending: Decision Review Officer (DRO) Election

    I see we received your Notice of Disagreement for (issue(s) on appeal) on (date of NOD), and you elected a review by a Decision Review Officer on (date of DRO election). Right now, your appeal is awaiting that review.
    If the Decision Review Officer determines we need additional information, we'll notify you to advise what is needed. If we don't need any additional information, the Decision Review Officer will make a decision, and you'll receive written notification.
    Unfortunately, we are unable to provide a timeframe as to when your appeal will be reviewed.
    PCRs should not advise that the case is "with a DRO" or that the appeal is "currently being worked" when the appeal is actually awaiting a DRO/De Novo review.
    Reminder: There is no timeframe for processing or completing appeals. PCRs should advise their caller that we are unable to provide a timeframe for processing appeals due to the complexity of each appeal. PCRs are not to use COVERS to provide appeal status or file location.
