DD-214 (I need a copy)

May I ask why you need a copy of your DD 214?

Will a "Statement of Service" letter suffice?

  • If yes, and service is verified, provide letter to address or fax number as caller instructs.
  • If no, or service not verified, and no folder is established, offer both of the following:
  • eVetRecs website: http://www.archives.gov/veterans/military-service-records/ AND
  • SF 180 and advise caller the return address is preprinted on the form.

If a folder is established and located at 376 (RMC), offer both of the following:

  • Provide RMC’s Customer Service Number 314-538-4500 AND offer to
  • Take a VAI, and notify caller that a copy may or may not be in the folder; give VAI reference number and update MAPD.
  • Note: If caller declines the two options above, offer the SF 180 and advise caller the return address is preprinted on the form.

If a folder is established and located at a VARO, check VirtualVA first. If present, print and forward to caller’s address or fax number as caller instructs. If no DD 214 is available,

  • initiate a VAI stating a copy of the DD 214 is needed and notify caller that a copy may or may not be in the folder. Update MAPD.
  • Offer both of the following
  • eVetRecs website: http://www.archives.gov/veterans/military-service-records/ AND
  • SF 180 and advise caller the return address is preprinted on the form.
